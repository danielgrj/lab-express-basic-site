Vaporwave

Vaporwave is a mellow, mesmerizing, and somewhat haunting aesthetic. The idea of vaporwave is being hard to understand, is taking what is familiar and make something new, something unique and different, but at the same time reminiscent of what it was. It's contradictory in nature, but still has a unified theme. For most people, it's something that you need to see in order to fully understand.
